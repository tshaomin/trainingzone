  ADRIENNE MICHOWSKI


•   an English mathematician and writer, chiefly known for her work on Charles Babbage's early mechanical general-purpose computer, the Analytical Engine.

•   Her notes on the engine include what is recognised as the first algorithm intended to be carried out by a machine.

Because of this, she is often regarded as the first computer programmer. She trains clients in programming and longevity.
